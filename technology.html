Handcrafted in Poland from European materials. We take craftsmanship seriously and look to nature for inspiration.

Woodcore

Spine of every kitesurf board. Cut, dried and assembled in own lumbermill.

Fibreglass

Flex and strength. Multi directional fiber orientation for best performance and weight.

Epoxy resins

High performance composite bond. Binds whole board in one piece.

Top and Base

Top and base sheet made from polyamid for best scratch resistance and UV protection.
